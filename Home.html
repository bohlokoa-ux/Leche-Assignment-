Afriski Resort, Lesotho

Afriski offers the opportunity to experience Lesotho's Maluti Mountains at any time of the year. It's an all-season destnation that offers exciting mountain activities, relaxation and a range of accomodation options to suit every holiday style.

'Maletsunyane Water Falls

It is one of the world's highest waterfalls and is important to Lesotho as a tourist attraction

Nelson Mandela Capture Site

The site is known as the Capture Site. The sculpture was created by the artist: 'Marco Cianfanelli'. It consists of 50 steel poles between 6 metres and 10 metres high. At a certain point, the 50 linear vertical steel columns line up, magically recreating and image of Nelson Mnadela's face.
